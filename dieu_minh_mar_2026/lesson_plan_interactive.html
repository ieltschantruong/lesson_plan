Vietnam CD-IELTS Format Constraints

This curriculum breaks away from legacy paper-based habits. The application below highlights the four critical environmental adjustments integrated into this 4-week plan. Click each card to reveal the pedagogical impact.

⏲ Listening Timing

No 10-Min Transfer

Impact: Practice strictly enforces a 2-minute review period. Students must type answers simultaneously while listening, predicting answers to manage cognitive load.

💻 Reading UI

Split-Screen Layout

Impact: All practice emphasizes side-by-side reading (text left, questions right). Students are trained to use digital highlight tools rather than underlining on paper.

⌨ Writing Mechanics

Digital Word Counts

Impact: Weekly dedicated writing lessons isolate typing speed, structural copy/paste editing, and utilizing the on-screen word count to avoid over-writing.

📚 Homework Rule

100% Digital Execution

Impact: Paper practice is banned. All self-study must occur on IDP/BC familiarization platforms to build crucial muscle memory for the testing interface.

Pedagogical Phasing

The 16 lessons are not randomly distributed. They follow a strict 4-phase psychological and academic progression. This chart illustrates the thematic weighting across the month.

  • ■
    Week 1: Foundations. Securing basic templates and easy points.
  • ■
    Week 2: Advanced. Complex methodologies and hard question types.
  • ■
    Week 3: Integration. Stamina building and strict timed tests.
  • ■
    Week 4: Simulation. Weakness targeting and psychological readiness.
