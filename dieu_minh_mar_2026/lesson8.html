Lesson 8: Half-Test Stamina Builder

Receptive Skills • 90 Minutes Total

Progress: 0 / 4

Goal

Build cognitive endurance by executing consecutive, strictly timed Reading and Listening sections on the computer interface without taking breaks.

Part 1: Homework Review (from Lesson 6) (00-10m)

Review Lesson 6 Assignments

Prior HW Links:
Reading P3 (8008) Reading P3 (8054) Vocab 6 List
  • Check Vocab: Do a rapid-fire verbal check of 5 random words from the Lesson 6 vocabulary list.
  • Review Reading Scores: Ask for their scores on the two Passage 3 homework assignments.
    • Check their accuracy on Yes/No/Not Given questions specifically. Did they confuse "No" with "Not Given"?
    • Did they finish within a reasonable timeframe, or did they run out of time?

Part 2: Reading Half-Test (10-50m)

Test Link Launchers

Launch Passage 2 (8855) Launch Passage 3 (7941)

1. Pre-Test Briefing (2 mins)

Explain the goal: "We are not pausing to review after Passage 2. You will do Passage 2 and Passage 3 back-to-back. You have exactly 38 minutes to finish both. Manage your own time. If a question is too hard, skip it and come back."

2. Live Execution (38 mins)

TIMER

Start a timer for exactly 38 minutes. Have the student share their screen if possible.

OBSERVE

Watch for "screen fatigue" (scrolling up and down aimlessly without reading). Remind them at the 18-minute mark that they should be moving on to Passage 3 soon.

💡

Tutor Tip: Do not interrupt them during this block. Take silent notes on which question types cause them to slow down the most.

Part 3: Listening Half-Test & Deep Review (50-85m)

Test Link Launchers

Launch Listening Part 2 (9971) Launch Listening Part 3 (8859)

1. Immediate Transition (15 mins)

Do not review the Reading test yet! Force them to switch brain contexts immediately to Listening, just like the real exam.

  • Open Listening Part 2. Play the audio. They must type simultaneously.
  • As soon as Part 2 finishes, immediately launch Part 3 and play the audio.

2. The "Error Analysis" Review Phase (20 mins)

Now that the stamina drill is over, grade both the Reading and Listening sections. Focus the remaining time on Error Analysis.

Reading Error Check

When reviewing incorrect answers, ask the student to categorize their mistake:

  • Type A: I didn't know the vocabulary.
  • Type B: I ran out of time/panicked.
  • Type C: I misread the question (e.g., missed the word "NOT").
  • Type D: I couldn't find the paragraph.
Listening Error Check

Deep dive into the transcript for missed questions in Part 3:

  • Did they fall for a distractor (Speaker A says X, Speaker B corrects them to Y)?
  • Did they fail to read the questions fully during the 30-second silent prep time?
  • Did they misspell a word while typing fast?

Part 4: Wrap-up & HW (85-90m)

1. Debrief (2 mins)

Congratulate the student on completing a tough, continuous block of testing. Reassure them that feeling exhausted after Reading Passage 3 and jumping into Listening is normal, and building this muscle memory is exactly why you did this drill.

2. Assign Homework (3 mins)

📖 Task: Full Reading Mock Test

Complete a full 60-minute Reading Test (Cambridge 20, Test 1) on the computer. Treat it like the real exam: no pausing, no dictionary, strictly 60 minutes.
