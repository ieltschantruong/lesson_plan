Open Live Cheat Sheet

Lexical Precision (6.0 vs 6.5)

Band 6.0 relies on general vocabulary (e.g., "very helpful", "bad problem"). Band 6.5 requires context-specific nouns and academic verbs (e.g., "highly beneficial", "profound obstacle").

Syntactic Flexibility

To hit 6.5, you must incorporate relative clauses, passive forms, and conditionals naturally. The examiner wants to see variable clause structures and accurate complex sentences.

Reading Speed Vectors

Moving to 6.5 requires stopping the word-for-word translation habit. Students must master scanning for synonyms and scanning for textual structures (like contrasts and consequences).

Reading Strategy & Live Drill

Breaking down logic structures and practicing Synonym-to-Synonym correspondence.

Question Guide Launch Drill Test 3

True / False / Not Given Logic Trap

To score 6.5, understand that FALSE means the text directly contradicts the assertion. NOT GIVEN means there is no logical way to verify the statement with the content provided. Don't guess or extrapolate.

Synonym & Paraphrasing Mechanism

Questions *never* use the same exact phrases as the passages. Find your keywords, anticipate synonyms (e.g., "overcome" vs "defeat"), and trace their matches directly to the sentence structure.

"Currently, I resideTo live in a particular place permanently or for an extended period. in Da Nang, a bustling coastal cityA very busy city located on the sea shore. in central Vietnam.
