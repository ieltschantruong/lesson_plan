Lesson 03: Vocalizing the Natural World & Great Escapes

Welcome to Lesson 3. Today, we anchor vocabulary to physical scenery. We will review geography, weather, holidays, and natural landscape materials. This helps her soft voice project with descriptive, sensory imagery, making speaking comfortable and beautiful.

Lesson Schedule (90 Minutes Total)

0/4 Completed
1

Geography & Weather Vocab Warmup (15 mins)

Using structural imagery to warm up tone range and voice resonance.

Coaching Tip:

  • Review standard and C1 geography words (mountain range, pristine beaches, rolling hills).
  • Instruct her to project her voice *wider* when talking about spacious geographical environments.
2

Holidays & Scenic Trips Practice (25 mins)

Speaking about personal escapes to bypass performance anxiety.

Methodology:

Talk about nature spots to soothe the nerves. Have her pick a familiar mountain or beach trip and describe the sensory details to keep her speech flowing easily.

3

Part 2 & 3: Nature & Geography Study (25 mins)

Tackling abstract environmental and landscape prompts logically.

Strategy:

"Anchor Vocabulary" + "Emotional connection" + "PREP Argumentative Structure"

4

Audio Capture & Tone Tuning Feedback (25 mins)

A low-pressure review focusing on her vowel length and projection strength.

Encouragement Rules:

Highlight **three major wins** (e.g., beautiful imagery, clear consonant sounds, correct vocabulary) before advising on **one** projection change.
