Lesson 01: Dissolving Barriers & Awakening the Voice

Welcome to your first session. Today is entirely about safety, breathing support, and vocal space. We will build a resonant vocal projection by centering your breathing, then introduce conversational expansion for IELTS Speaking Part 1.

Lesson Schedule (90 Minutes Total)

0/5 Completed
1

Anxiety Relief & Vocal Alignment (15 mins)

Introduction, relaxing posture, aligning nervous systems.

Tutor Checklist:

  • Unpack anxiety triggers (e.g. eye contact, fear of pauses).
  • Begin sitting comfortably with shoulders back to allow open diaphragmatic access.
2

Diaphragmatic Breathing & The "Hmm" Hum (20 mins)

Unlocking abdominal support and resonance.

Action Plan:

First, breathe in through your nose for 4 seconds, then out humming "Hmm..." focusing on feeling vibration in your collarbones.

3

Part 1 Framework - No More "Yes/No" (25 mins)

Introduction to natural structural extension.

Core Speaking Frame:

"Yes/No answer" + "Reason" + "Vivid personal detail/Example."

Example: "Do you like quiet places?" → "Absolutely, yes... because quiet rooms help me process my thoughts... I love sitting in my bedroom, reading with soft music playing."

4

Integrated Reading & Shadowing Flow (15 mins)

Reading aloud to internalize natural pacing and pauses.

Method:

The tutor reads a descriptive paragraph beautifully. The student reads immediately after (Shadowing), focusing on breath marks, pause boundaries, and stressing important verbs.

5

First Practice & Safe Review (15 mins)

Low-stakes recording, highlighting positive elements first.

Encouragement Rule:

Highlight **three outstanding things** she did right (e.g. gorgeous vocabulary, precise grammar, nice chest resonance) before giving **one single direction** on projection improvement.
