Speaking Part 1

Topic: Accessories & Footwear (Practice)

❓

IELTS Questions

🕶️ Sunglasses

  1. Do people in your country wear sunglasses?
  2. Where can you buy sunglasses?
  3. Do you like to wear sunglasses?

⌚ Watches

  1. How often do you wear a watch?
  2. What kind of watches do you like to wear?
  3. Do people wear watches in your country?
  4. Did you receive any watch as a gift when you were a child?

👟 Shoes

  1. What’s your favorite type of shoes?
  2. Do you know anyone who likes to buy a lot of shoes?
  3. Have you ever bought shoes online?
  4. How often do you buy shoes?
💡 View Sunglasses Sample Answers ▼

1. Do people in your country wear sunglasses?

Yes, definitely. We live in a tropical climate where the intense sunlight during the summer can be ruthless, so the masses wear sunglasses daily. I am no exception; they are an essential, all-purpose accessory to shield our eyes while driving on hot days.

2. Where can you buy sunglasses?

You can buy them almost anywhere, from local street markets to premium boutiques that showcase extravagant designer brands. However, if you are looking for something chic and durable, specialty optical stores are the best place to find certified UV protection.

3. Do you like to wear sunglasses?

Absolutely, I love wearing them. Not only do they protect my eyes, but they also complement my daily outfit perfectly. I usually choose a frame shape that is flattering for my face to give me a subtle, daring style.

💡 View Watches Sample Answers ▼

1. How often do you wear a watch?

I wear a watch on a daily basis. I consider myself quite punctual, almost like a slave to my schedule. I usually strap on a highly functional smart watch with a comfortable rubber watch strap before heading out of the house.

2. What kind of watches do you like to wear?

I personally prefer a classic analogue watch over a standard digital watch. I love admiring the glistening metal frame and the highly intricate details on the watch dial. To me, it feels like wearing a small mechanical masterpiece.

3. Do people wear watches in your country?

Yes, wearing a watch is still incredibly popular. While younger people use them to track health metrics, older generations wear classic designs as symbols of elegance and premium workmanship. It graces their wrists beautifully on formal occasions.

4. Did you receive any watch as a gift when you were a child?

Yes, I did. I vividly remember receiving a colorful, water-resistant watch for my tenth birthday. I wore it everywhere until it began to show signs of wear (damage) years later, but it holds great sentimental value for me.

💡 View Shoes Sample Answers ▼

1. What’s your favorite type of shoes?

My absolute favorite type is sneakers because they are incredibly comfortable. While I occasionally wear boots or sandals for formal events, a good pair of sneakers is my go-to for daily walking because they have a stretchy and supportive sole.

2. Do you know anyone who likes to buy a lot of shoes?

Yes, my sister is a prime example. She is completely obsessed with footwear, especially designer high heels and flats. She has a massive collective of shoes in her wardrobe, and her passion for shopping is truly extravagant.

3. Have you ever bought shoes online?

Yes, I have, but only for simple styles like slip-ons or everyday flip flops where the sizing is very forgiving. Buying performance shoes online can be risky because you can't test if they are truly all-purpose or if they fit correctly without trying them on.

4. How often do you buy shoes?

I only buy shoes once or twice a year. I try to cultivate a minimalist lifestyle, so I only replace my footwear when my current pairs show major signs of wear and can no longer be repaired.

Speaking Part 2

Topic: Special Occasion Clothing (Cue Card)

👔
📝

IELTS Cue Card

Describe a piece of clothes that you wear on a special occasion.

You should say:

  • What it is
  • When you bought it
  • How often you wear it
  • And explain why you like it.
💡 View Band 6.0+ Sample Answer ▼
"I'd like to talk about a custom-tailored suit that I reserve specifically for formal events.

I purchased it about two years ago, right before my university graduation ceremony. Instead of buying something off the rack, I went to an experienced tailor in my city because I wanted an outfit that fit me perfectly. It’s a deep navy blue suit made from a lightweight, breathable fabric.

Since it's highly formal, I don't wear it very often. I usually only take it out of my closet for major events, like weddings, job interviews, or important banquets. I’d say I wear it maybe three or four times a year at most.

There are a few reasons why I absolutely love this suit. Firstly, the dark blue color makes it incredibly elegant and versatile; it pairs well with almost any shirt or tie. Secondly, because it was custom-made for my measurements, it flatters my body shape and is much more comfortable than standard store-bought clothes. Whenever I put it on, I instantly feel a surge of confidence and professionalism. It never fails to make a good impression on special occasions."
