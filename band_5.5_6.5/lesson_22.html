Speaking Part 1

Topic: Celebrities (Practice)

📸

IELTS Questions

  1. Who is your favorite celebrity in Vietnam?
  2. Do you like any foreign celebrities?
  3. Would you want to be a celebrity in the future?
  4. Do you think we should protect famous people's privacy?
  5. How do celebrities influence their fans?
💡 View Band 6.0+ Sample Answers ▼

1. Who is your favorite celebrity in Vietnam?

My absolute favorite is Ha Anh Tuan. He's a true A-lister and a global household name for Vietnamese people abroad. I admire him because he has maintained an untarnished reputation throughout his entire career.

2. Do you like any foreign celebrities?

Yes, I really like Emma Watson. She is a highly versatile artist who can act, model, and speak publicly about important issues. She also seems incredibly resilient when facing public scrutiny.

3. Would you want to be a celebrity in the future?

Not at all. Even though rising to stardom brings wealth, I know that prominent people are often under tremendous pressure. I wouldn't want the public to constantly gossip about my personal life.

4. Do you think we should protect famous people's privacy?

Absolutely. Just because someone has a magnetic personality and works in entertainment doesn't give reporters the right to illegally invade their privacy. They deserve personal space like everyone else.

5. How do celebrities influence their fans?

They have a massive impact. Because they are always in the spotlight, they can easily set new fashion or lifestyle trends. However, if they make a mistake, they immediately come in for a lot of harsh criticism from fans.

Speaking Part 2

Topic: Famous Person (Cue Card)

🌟
📝

IELTS Cue Card

Describe a famous person you are interested in.

You should say:

  • Who that person is
  • When you first knew that person
  • What this person is famous for
  • And explain why you admire this person.
💡 View Band 6.0+ Sample Answer ▼
"I'd like to talk about Keanu Reeves, a highly prominent actor in Hollywood. He is a very well-known figure globally, mostly recognized for starring in massive blockbuster movies like The Matrix and John Wick.

I first knew about him when I was a teenager. I watched The Matrix on TV and was completely captivated by his performance. Since then, he has consistently been in the spotlight, not just for his acting, but for his lifestyle.

What he is most famous for, outside of his films, is his humble and grounded personality. Unlike many celebrities who are notorious for bad behavior or who complain about an invasion of privacy, Keanu is often praised by the paparazzi and fans for being exceptionally kind and down-to-earth.

The main reason I idolize him is because he is an excellent role model. Even though he makes headlines and has numerous brand endorsements, he donates a lot of his wealth to charity quietly. He proves that you can achieve global fame and still remain a good person."
