Speaking Part 1

Topic: Family (Practice)

👪

IELTS Questions

  1. How often do you meet your family?
  2. What activities do your family like doing together?
  3. How has your family influenced you?
  4. Why do some people today choose not to get married?
💡 View Band 6.0+ Sample Answers ▼

1. How often do you meet your family?

I currently live with my parents, so I see them every day. On weekends, we usually have a small family gathering where we eat dinner and watch a movie together.

2. What activities do your family like doing together?

Because we are all on the same wavelength, we love discussing current events and politics. A love for lively debate is definitely a trait that runs in our family.

3. How has your family influenced you?

They gave me a wonderful upbringing. I think I take after my mother in terms of my patience, and I definitely inherit my father's sense of humor. Even when we quarrel, we always make up quickly.

4. Why do some people today choose not to get married?

Many young people are prioritizing their careers over settling down. They might also want to avoid the financial burden of giving a child a good upbringing or fear giving their partner the silent treatment when issues arise.

Speaking Part 1

Topic: Teachers (Practice)

👩‍🏫

IELTS Questions

  1. Do you like any teachers?
  2. Do you want to be a teacher?
  3. What qualities should a good teacher have?
  4. Is having a good teacher important for students? Why/ Why not?
💡 View Band 6.0+ Sample Answers ▼

1. Do you like any teachers?

Yes, my high school math teacher was my favorite. She was incredibly approachable and supportive, always willing to help us after class if we didn't understand something.

2. Do you want to be a teacher?

Honestly, no. To be a good teacher, you need to be very methodical and diligent with grading and lesson planning. I don't think I have the necessary patience for that profession.

3. What qualities should a good teacher have?

A great educator should definitely be conscientious and tactful when giving feedback. They should also know how to design interactive and student-centered lessons to successfully arouse students' interest in the subject.

4. Is having a good teacher important for students? Why/ Why not?

Absolutely. Teachers spend a vast amount of time with young people, so they often serve as a prime role model. If a teacher is on good terms with their class, it drastically improves the students' motivation and academic performance.
