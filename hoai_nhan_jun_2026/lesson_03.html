Lesson Itinerary

1. Homework & Warm-up 15m

Review student's recording submissions on Planning, Morning Routines, and Being Busy from Lesson 02. Audit filler occurrence rates.

2. Connected Speech Theory 30m

Train in the mechanics of liaison (consonant-to-vowel links, e.g., "rush hour" as /rʌʃaʊər/), intrusive /j/ or /w/, and weak form contractions. Practice utilizing Snacks vocabulary.

3. Rhythmic Commute Drills 35m

Run continuous speech flow tests using Public Transport contexts. Direct candidate's breathing points to sync perfectly with clause-boundaries rather than halting inside semantic units.

4. Homework Prep & Setups 10m

Establish parameters for the Learnivo daily routine shadowing assignment, demonstrating pitch/stress contours.

Specialized Audio Shadowing Workout

Syllable vs Stress-Timed Practice

Open Shadowing Platform
Phase 1: Passive Analysis

Open the practice module and play the audio sample. Rather than looking at the text, focus exclusively on the rising/falling intonation peaks and the weak form reductions of grammatical particles (like "to", "for", "and").

Phase 2: Dual Vocalization

Speak aloud concurrently with the voice recording, maintaining a latency gap of under 0.5 seconds. Replicate the vocal contours, connected liaisons, and rhythmic breathing spots to match the native model cadence.

Phase 3: Unassisted Review

Record your individual response without the audio track playing. Analyze whether you are rushing or if you maintained stress-timed prominence over high-value lexical terms.
