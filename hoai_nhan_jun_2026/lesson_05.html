Lesson Itinerary

1. Weekend Speed Warm-Up 15m

Deploy rapid response exercises on weekend routines to spark immediate, low-latency vocal mechanics.

2. Synonym Bridges & Memory 25m

Focus on structural memory. Combine vocabulary with transitions like "If memory serves me right..." to construct longer responses.

3. Continuous Stamina Trials 40m

Complete several 60-second speech endurance runs over weekends and holidays, focusing on maintaining rhythm.

4. Handover to Part 2 Arc 10m

Transition into Part 2 narrative frameworks. Explore homework prompt cues, structuring strategies, and visual brainstorming.

Part 2 Narrative Transition
Topic 1 Road Trip

Interesting Vehicle Trip

"Describe a car, motorbike, or bicycle trip you think would be interesting."

  • Where you want to go
  • Who you want to go with
  • How easy or difficult the trip would be
  • Explain why using this vehicle for the trip would be interesting
Lexical Targets: Scenic routes, long-haul drives, rugged terrain, navigate, self-reliance, custom pace, flexibility.
Topic 2 Serene Space

Quiet Place Visit

"Describe a place you enjoyed visiting that was very quiet."

  • When you went there
  • Where it is and why you wanted to visit
  • What you will do when you go there
  • Explain why you enjoyed visiting this quiet place
Lexical Targets: Tranquil, sanctuary, peaceful retreat, escape, unwind, silence, pristine, solitude, rustling leaves.
Test 15 Megacity

Favorite City Visited

"Describe a city that you have been to and enjoy visiting."

  • Where the city is
  • What you did there
  • How many times you have been there
  • Explain why you really enjoyed visiting this city
Lexical Targets: Metropolis, vibrant culture, landmarks, bustling streets, urban layout, architecture, culinary scenes.
