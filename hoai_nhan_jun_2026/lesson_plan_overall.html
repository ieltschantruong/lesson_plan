Methodology

Core Fluency Protocols

01

The 4-3-2 Method

Paul Nation Applied Linguistics

Students deliver a cue card in 4 minutes, immediate repeat in 3 minutes, and final iteration in 2 minutes. Eliminates structural cognitive load, locking focus onto physical pacing and rhythmic momentum.

02

Rapid-Fire Q&A

PTE Speaking Drills

10-15 straightforward prompts asked in immediate succession. The candidate has exactly 1 second to begin speaking. Forces her logic-driven mind to bypass over-calculation and build reflex patterns.

03

Connected Speech

Shadowing & Linking

Systematic shadowing of high-scoring mock exams. Focuses on link transitions (consonant-to-vowel) and weak form contractions, eliminating typical staccato rhythm barriers.

04

"Pause & Breathe"

Filler Mitigation Protocol

Replaces cultural stalling sounds ("eto/ano") with structured silence or elite native transitional fillers. Preserves the coherence band criteria, keeping physical breathing synced with dynamic speech output.
